About Institute

The Institute is engaged in the education, training & rehabilitation of mentally challenged & handicapped children. The Institute was founded on 01/07/2002 to improve the intelligence level to teach educational scale to give experience of real pleasure of living, to achieve the right & equal opportunities & protection for the mentally challenged handicapped. On 22/12/2004 Govt. of Maharashtra sanctioned our Special School for mentally challenged on permanently non-grantable basis.
Our Institute in engaged in the field of special Education, Vocational training, Development & research activities. The Institute was inaugurated in the year 2002 with the strength of 3 students which has on date is 30. This shows that the society is in need of such activities. Special teacher to teach the prescribed syllabus, Art teacher to take care & explore their inbuilt art of games, singing music etc. One Asst. teacher & one care taker are employed to get the best out of the students.
The reputed Medical practitioners viz Hon. Psychologist, Dr. Suresh Naik, Hon speech therapist, Dr. Ramesh Naik, Hon Medical officer & Psychiatrist. Dr. Mrs. Bargale, visited our Institute for Medical checkup.
On 27/10/2002 Institute had arranged a free camp for medical checkup for mentally Retarded & Handicapped children. 100 students took part in the medical camp. Institute calibrates various Indian festivals in its campus to create awareness of Indian Culture. All national important days are celebrated such as Independence Day, Republic Day, World Disabled Day, and World Mentally Retardation Day.
