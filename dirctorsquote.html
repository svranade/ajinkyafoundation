Trustee Says :

Sou. Shailaja Ashok Goundaje
DEE., DSE. (MR)
Trustee : Ajinkya Foundation
Ex. Director : Institute For the Mentally Handicapped, Kupawad.
I, Shailaja Ashok Goundaje, completed my Diploma in Electrical Engineering and got married. I was blessed with three children who were completely normal at the time of birth. However, after some period, all the three children were caught by fever and had fits followed by development of mentally retardedness one after another. I was totally collapsed by the psychological shock due to this calamity. I could not understand why the destiny is playing with such a hide and seek game. I lost all hope worth living and there was a big question of how to provide care to all my three children. There was no proper treatment for such children in society. Society considered them outcast and neglected them. In such a situation how to face the society as mother of three mentally retarded children. Our family life also got disturbed due to this new responsibility. As if there was some dark evil shadow hovering over our family. While struggling in such darkness of frustration, I saw a new ray of hope in the form of Wai Training Centre of Wai Akshar Institute. This was an institute for giving comprehensive training of how to take care and handle mentally retarded children. The training course was of one year duration. To strengthen my children psychologically to enable them to live normal life in society and to extend this help to other such children from society, I completed DSE (MR) training course in 2002. As per my many aspirations my objective and dream was to start a special school in Kupwad for mentally retarded children. It was a very difficult task. There was problem of financial support and land for school. However, as I was firm in my decision, I decided to start the school in our own house for convenience.

Today, society looks to the mentally retarded children by scornful eyes. However, mentally retarded child is not psychic. The greatest punishment to parents in the world is birth of mentally retarded child and its care taking. If these children get proper guidance through special school, they can live normal life. Even after parents expire, these children can live normal life with necessary protection in residential school. And specifically with this objective, a special school under the auspices of Ajinkya Foundation, was started for mentally retarded children on 1st July 2002.

There were many difficulties before me. Generally the parents are not ready to send their mentally retarded children to school. Because they do not believe that their child will improve. Hence I visited all parents of such children in Kupwad area and convinced them about necessity of special school and prompted them for sending their children to school.
The school which was started only with my own three children on 1st July 2002, is now teaching 30 children. With the help of 7 staff, the small plant of this school is making progress in development and training of these children.
Thank you,
Sou. Shailaja Ashok Goundaje.
