After getting Degree of Diploma in Electrical Engineering, I married with Shri. Ashok Goundaje in due course 3 children were born. Initially all was normal but unfortunately all three ware mentally retarded due to conversions in feyer which mentally shocked us & disturbed my normal life. As explained in my husband's speech. I decided to make my children capable mentally & physically to live their normal life. With this sole intention on 25 th June 2001 I joined Wai Training center Which is well recognized for special education on how to handle & make capable Mentally retarded children. I passed with destination,

On getting the degree I,with my husband started a school for mentally & physically handicapped children in my house at Kupwad. which is the only school of such type in surrounding area. To my surprise. the initial strength of the 3 students rose to 30 students in the span of 3 years. Encouraged I appointed two qualified teachers along with     

The only & sole object of our institution is children who are mentally & physically handicapped should lead their normal life accepting the challenged of the destiny & to train the respective parents accordingly.
